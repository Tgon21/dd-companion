# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 30, 2026_

This app runs **entirely on your own computer**. It has no accounts, no analytics, no trackers, and no server that the developer controls or can see.

## What's stored, and where

- **On your device only.** Your collection, lineup, criteria, and view settings are saved in your browser's local storage. The card & price database is cached by the local server on your machine. None of it is sent to the developer or any third party.
- **No personal data is collected.** The developer never receives, stores, or has access to anything you do in this app.

## Network requests

- The app talks to **mlb26.theshow.com** only — to read the public card/price data and, if you choose, your own inventory. These requests go from **your machine** to The Show's servers; nothing is routed through a server we operate.
- **Session cookie (Advanced import):** if you paste it, it is held in memory for a single request, used only to ask theshow.com for your inventory, and then discarded. It is **never written to disk, logged, or transmitted anywhere except theshow.com**.
- Card images load directly from The Show's servers.

## Third parties

MLB The Show and theshow.com are operated by Sony Interactive Entertainment / San Diego Studio and are governed by **their own privacy policy**, which covers your account and any data on their site. This app is not affiliated with them.

## Your control

You can wipe everything this app stores at any time with **Clear my collection** and by clearing this site's browser storage. Closing/removing the local app removes the local cache.

Questions or concerns: open an issue on this repository.
