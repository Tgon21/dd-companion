# Lineup Builder guide

The Lineup Builder assembles a full team **from the cards you own**, optionally narrowed to a
program's or event's requirements. This covers everything except the filtering switches — those
have their own page: [Criteria guide (ALL / ANY)](CRITERIA-GUIDE.md).

## The board
- **Field** — the diamond: C, 1B, 2B, 3B, SS, LF, CF, RF, plus **DH** and the **P** (pitcher) spot.
- **Rotation** — 5 starting-pitcher slots.
- **Bullpen** — 8 relief slots.
- **Bench** — 4 reserve slots with tactical roles (see below).
- **Spare players** — every other owned card, ranked by how well it fits your criteria.

## Auto-build
Click **Auto-build** and it fills the team from your collection:
- **Fielders** go to spots they're eligible for, using each card's **primary and secondary** positions; **DH** takes the best remaining hitter.
- **Rotation** takes SP-eligible pitchers; the **bullpen** takes RP/CP-eligible ones — best fit first.
- **Fill pitchers first** (toggle) does the staff before the field.
- Manual placement has **no restrictions** — you can drag any card anywhere; dropping on an occupied slot **swaps** the two.

## Batting order (sabermetric)
The order follows *The Book*: **#2** your best overall hitter, **#4 (cleanup)** and **#5** your top
power, **#1** the highest on-base + speed table-setter, **#3** the next-best bat, **6–8** descending,
**#9** a second leadoff. Drag the rows to reorder, or use the ▲▼ buttons.

- **🔀 Stagger batting hands** (on by default): avoids **3 same-handed hitters in a row** (2 is fine;
  switch hitters break a run). It makes the smallest swaps needed, so the sabermetric order stays mostly intact.

## Programs grind vs Events
- **🏆 Programs grind** — matching **more** criteria is better; cards rank by how many they fit.
- **⚡ Events** — you only need to fit **one** criterion; among those that qualify, the **best player (OVR)** is chosen.

## Bench reserves (tactical roles)
**🪑 Fill bench** stocks the 4 reserves from your criteria-matching cards not already starting, picked **within a sensible OVR band** then by the best role stat:
- **PR** — pinch runner (speed / steal),
- **PH** — power pinch-hitter, picked to give a **platoon** bat vs your starters,
- **UTIL** — defensive utility (multi-position, switch-hitter preferred),
- **C** — backup catcher (so you can safely pinch-run/hit for your starting C).

Use the **Fill bench** button, or tick the checkbox so Auto-build fills it too.

## Saving, undo, quick-fill
- **💾 Save filter/build** stores your **criteria together with** the field, batting order, rotation
  and bullpen. Load any saved setup from the dropdown (or 🗑 to delete). Saved in your browser.
- **Undo / Redo** walk back the last 10 changes.
- **⚡ Quick-fill** on a spare player drops it into the correct open slot for its position.
- **Only show matches** (above the spare list) hides cards that don't qualify for your criteria.
