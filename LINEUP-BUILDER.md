# The Lineup Builder tab

The Lineup Builder is the heart of the app: assemble a **full team from the cards you own** and
tune it to whatever a program or event is asking for. It's a granular lineup creator — you set the
requirements, it ranks and places your players.

There are two sides to it, each with its own guide:

- **[Lineup builder guide](LINEUP-GUIDE.md)** — *building the team*: auto-build, the diamond/rotation/
  bullpen/bench, the sabermetric batting order, handedness staggering, Programs vs Events modes,
  bench roles, saving builds, undo/redo, and quick-fill.
- **[Criteria guide (ALL / ANY)](CRITERIA-GUIDE.md)** — *choosing the right cards*: the two-level
  ALL/ANY filtering that decides which of your cards qualify, e.g.
  `(Pitcher AND Sinker AND Cutter) AND (35+ OR Vintage OR Live)`.

## Quick orientation
- **Field** — the diamond (C, 1B, 2B, 3B, SS, LF, CF, RF) plus **DH** and the **P** spot.
- **Rotation / Bullpen** — 5 starters and 8 relievers.
- **Bench** — 4 tactical reserves (pinch runner, pinch hitter, utility, backup catcher).
- **Spare players** — every other owned card, ranked by how well it fits your criteria.

New here? Start with the **[Lineup builder guide](LINEUP-GUIDE.md)** to build a team, then use the
**[Criteria guide](CRITERIA-GUIDE.md)** to dial in exactly which cards qualify.
